French Language Instruction

I am a French language instructor with a PhD in French & Francophone Studies. I design communicative, student-centered courses that help students build confidence, fluency, and cultural awareness across levels.

Teaching Philosophy

In my courses, students speak, listen, read, and write from the first day. I build structured, interactive classrooms where students use French for real communication rather than isolated drills.

Assessment

I use transparent rubrics to assess speaking and writing fairly, focusing on comprehensibility, accuracy, range, and fluency. Feedback is specific and actionable so students know how to improve.

Curriculum Design

I design courses as part of a coherent sequence in which each level prepares students for the next, using backward design to support long-term success.
